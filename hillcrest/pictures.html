 Hokies For the Hungry: Campus Blitz 2006

The band runs this project to raise food for the Montgomery County Christmas Store every year. They have two phases, one at the Maroon Effect game (this year versus Clemson) of "a can from every fan" (that's 66,233 cans) and the other being the Campus Blitz. For the past two years, Hillcrest has collected the most of any team (they let us work together because of the small number of residents) and won the grand prize of an ice cream party. Maybe we will again this year with our 600+ cans.
