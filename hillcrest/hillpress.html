Hillcrest: The Hillpress

Starting in 2005, the RAs in Hillcrest published a rather comical monthly newsletter. The following year, the newsletter recieved a boost and some legitimacy by becoming an official class - an Honors Reading Group, to be precise. Hillpress now has over twice as many staffers, giving us an excuse to publish it weekly.

It was thought that the first year of issues had been lost to posterity, however, a recent donation by an anonomous Hillpress collector has completed our collection. Therefore, we can now present every single article ever featured in Hillpress in as PDFs.
